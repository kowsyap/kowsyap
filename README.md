## 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm a Computer Engineering MS student at **George Mason University** (GPA: 3.89/4.0), specializing in **FPGA-based hardware design, RTL design, and hardware-software co-design**. My current research focuses on implementing **post-quantum cryptography algorithms in hardware**, targeting Xilinx Artix-7 and Zynq SoC platforms. I also serve as a Graduate Teaching Assistant for Computer Architecture and Digital System Design, mentoring ~150 students in Verilog/VHDL and FPGA implementation workflows.

Previously, I worked as a **Digital Design Engineer at Infosys**, designing RTL modules and FPGA-based hardware accelerators using Verilog/VHDL and verifying them with SystemVerilog/UVM. I hold a Bachelor's in Electronics Engineering from **IIIT Design & Manufacturing Kancheepuram (IIITDMK)**.
